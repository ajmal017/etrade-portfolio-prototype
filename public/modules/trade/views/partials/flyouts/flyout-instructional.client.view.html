Bracketed
{{transaction.conditional.tempType}}

Bracketed {{transaction.conditional.tempType}} orders are designed to help limit your loss and lock in a profit by "bracketing" an order with two opposite-side orders. A BUY order is bracketed by a high-side sell limit order and a low-side sell stop order. A SELL order is bracketed by a high-side buy stop order and a low side buy limit order.
